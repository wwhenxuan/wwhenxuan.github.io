I graduated from the School of Artificial Intelligence in [Xidian University](https://www.xidian.edu.cn/) and am currently pursuing a master's degree in School of Telecommunications Engineering.

My research interests include Time Series Analysis (TSA), Signal Processing, Complex System Modeling and Symbolic Regression.

![background](../images/photos/background.png)

I'm captivated by **time series** in all forms, which intricate dances of data that unveil the hidden rhythms of **complex dynamical systems**. Each time series whisper the story of its creator: perhaps a high-order linear systems, a nonlinear systems, or a chaotic. When you trace their essence through differential equations, you cannot escape the haunting beauty of chaos and fractals, a universe where order and turbulence waltz in eternal paradox.
